SICO (Student Integrated Committee)

The Student Integrated Committee has a large number of student-initiated clubs for the development of personality, managerial knowledge and skills, values, and ethics. These qualities are necessary for making an honorable living and contributing to the socioeconomic development and welfare of society. The SICO committee works in coordination with the Literary and Cultural Committee.

Objectives:

  1. Induction of 'service' in education for imparting 'education for total transformation'.
  2. Development of integrated manpower with technological and managerial knowledge and skills, values and ethics to contribute to the socio-economic welfare of society.
  3. Providing basic infrastructure to old-age homes, orphanages, asylums, and blind schools.
  4. Propagating the ideals and benefits of 'right to education' to students of remote areas.
  5. Enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of technical education and welfare activities.
  6. Organizing workshops and seminars.

Various Student Initiated Clubs under SICO:

CLUB ENDEAVOR

Aim: The club is a student-initiated literary club, aiming at sharpening literary skills among students.
Objectives:
  • To familiarize students with the competitive environment of the real world.
  • To equip students with vocal and written techniques essential for success in today’s competitive environment.
  • To enhance interaction between students from different branches.
  • To foster creativity in young minds.
Activities:

The club organizes literary events preparing students to participate in inter-collegiate festivals, developing leadership and team spirit for future endeavors.

RED ANTS

Aim: The club aims to nurture fine arts skills in students through activities like pencil shading, mehendi, and t-shirt painting.
Objectives:
  • To expose students to real-world challenges.
  • To unite students through cultural activities.
  • To encourage creativity and hidden talents.
Activities:

The club organizes various art activities fostering creativity and overall development of students.

CLUB INSPIRAZE

Aim: To inspire students through enlightening speeches delivered by professionals.
Objectives:
  • To make every student an achiever.
  • To help students discover their true interests.
  • To aid students in choosing role models.
Activities:

The club arranges guest lectures by professionals to build emotional character and inspire students.

CLUB WALTZ

Aim: To provide a platform for students interested in dance.
Objectives:
  • To promote team spirit and competition.
  • To encourage discussions to enhance dance skills.
Activities:

The club organizes dance competitions, encouraging participation at state and national levels.
