Cultural Activities

This committee regularly conducts various cultural and co-curricular activities for the students of RVR&JC CE in order to exhibit their talents. These programs are held in Sri. G. Ramarayudu Auditorium, Open Air Theatre (OAT), and Silver Jubilee Block Seminar Hall. The various events conducted under the committee are:

  1. Singing - Classical, Light, Folk (Solo & Group)
  2. Dance - Classical, Folk, Western (Solo & Group)
  3. Instrumental - Flute, Tabla, Piano, Veena, etc.
  4. Mono Action
  5. Miming
  6. Dialogue Delivery
  7. Skit
  8. Mimicry
  9. Choreography
  10. Traditional Show
  11. Czars
  12. Drama
  13. Action - Over Action
  14. Any Creative Entertainment Event
  15. Technical Working Models
  16. Inspirational Talks
  17. Quizzes
